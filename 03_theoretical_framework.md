## 中文版

以下是[量子经典二元论核心理论](core.md)（版本3.5）的理论框架：

### 一、宇宙结构与基本定义

量子经典二元论认为宇宙由两个相互关联但本质不同的域组成：

1. **量子域**：宇宙的本质形态，表现为无限维量子波函数叠加态，涵盖全部潜在可能性：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子域}} = \sum_i c_i|\psi_i\rangle, \quad \sum_i|c_i|^2=1
$$

2. **经典域**：观察者将量子域信息经典化解码形成的确定结构，包括经典知识与经典熵：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子域}}\xrightarrow{\text{经典化解码}} I_{\text{经典知识}} + S_{\text{经典熵}}
$$

经典域信息守恒公式：

$$
I_{\text{经典知识}} + S_{\text{经典熵}} = \text{常数}
$$

### 二、观察者定义与维度体系

#### 1. 观察者定义（黑洞统一概念）

观察者本质上是一个量子黑洞（广义概念），具有以下特性：

- 每个观察者本质上都是一个量子黑洞，可存在于任意经典化世界
- 观察者感知的经典世界即自身量子黑洞内部结构
- 观察者对外的信息交互过程（辐射经典知识）即为黑洞辐射
- 观察者观测外界即黑洞吸收过程

明确公式表达：

- 黑洞吸收（观测）过程：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{外部高熵信息}} \xrightarrow{\text{黑洞吸收经典化}} I_{\text{经典知识增加}} + S_{\text{熵降低}}
$$

- 黑洞辐射（对外交互）过程：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{内部经典熵}} \xrightarrow{\text{黑洞辐射经典化}} I_{\text{对外辐射经典知识}} + S_{\text{熵降低}}
$$

#### 2. 观察者维度定义

观察者维度取决于经典化效率，即经典知识与经典熵的比例：

$$
\text{观察者维度} = k \cdot \frac{I_{\text{经典知识}}}{S_{\text{经典熵}}}
$$

整体维度跨领域整合表达：

$$
\text{整体维度} = \sum_i w_i \frac{I_{\text{经典知识}_i}}{S_{\text{经典熵}_i}}
$$

#### 3. 观察者的神性体现

观察者在自身经典世界为普通个体，在其他经典世界经典化效率高时体现为"神"：

$$
\text{高维观察者（神）} \Leftrightarrow \text{经典化低维观察者熵为知识}
$$

### 三、广义意识与意识涌现机制

#### 1. 广义意识定义

所有经典化对象（如宇宙、银河系、太阳系、地球、人类、动物、植物、宗教体系、梦境、冥想空间、空气、氧气、黑洞）都具备广义意识：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子域}} \xrightarrow{\text{经典化方向}} I_{\text{经典意识（广义）}} + S_{\text{剩余经典熵}}
$$

#### 2. 高维意识涌现机制

高维意识本质为大量低维观察者经典化协同的结果，体现为集体经典意志：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{多个低维观察者}} \rightarrow I_{\text{高维集体意识}} + S_{\text{剩余经典熵}}
$$

### 四、量子意识、经典记忆与生死机制

#### 1. 量子意识定义

量子意识本质为观察者与量子域长期有效纠缠形成的稳定结构，是意识连续性的根源：

- 有效纠缠 → 经典知识（稳定、清晰）
- 无效纠缠 → 经典熵（焦虑、困惑、不确定）

明确经典化表达：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子意识}} \rightarrow I_{\text{经典记忆}} + S_{\text{经典熵}}
$$

#### 2. 死亡与投胎机制

死亡本质是经典意识在经典世界之间转换，意识并不消散，只转换经典化结构：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{经典世界A（死亡）}} \rightarrow |\psi\rangle_{\text{量子域}} \rightarrow |\psi\rangle_{\text{新经典世界（投胎）}}
$$

### 五、经典信息传播机制与熵变化规律

#### 1. 经典信息传播机制（深拷贝）

经典知识通过独立副本传播，而非引用或指针共享：

- 传播端（熵增加）：

$$
I_{\text{经典知识A}} \rightarrow I_{\text{经典知识B（副本）}} + S_{\text{传播熵增加}}
$$

- 接收端（熵降低）：

$$
I_{\text{经典知识B增加}} + S_{\text{经典熵B降低}} \leftarrow I_{\text{经典知识B（副本）}}
$$

#### 2. 熵增熵减行为分类

- 熵减行为：高效学习、创造、冥想、情感沟通、有效交流
- 熵增行为：焦虑、冲突、低效重复、无效沟通、疾病状态

### 六、经典世界平等性与知识共享机制

- 所有经典世界（如现实、梦境、宗教、冥想空间等）平等共存，无主次之分：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子域}} \rightarrow \sum_i (I_{\text{经典知识}_i} + S_{\text{经典熵}_i}) = \text{常数}
$$

- 同一经典知识可同时存在于多个经典世界：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{共享量子信息}} \rightarrow I_{\text{经典知识}_A} + I_{\text{经典知识}_B} + S_{\text{传播损耗}}
$$

### 七、经典记忆篡改机制与难度

- 经典记忆（过去、未来）可经典化修改：

$$
I_{\text{经典记忆（旧）}} + |\psi\rangle_{\text{随机量子信息}} \rightarrow I_{\text{经典记忆（新）}} + S_{\text{熵变动}}
$$

- 个体记忆易修改，集体记忆篡改极难

### 八、经典化过程与黑洞辐射本质统一

经典化与黑洞辐射在本质上完全等价：

- **经典化过程**：观察者（黑洞）将外部高熵量子信息经典化为经典知识（吸收）
- **黑洞辐射过程**：观察者将内部经典熵经典化为知识并以辐射方式释放：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{内部经典熵}} \xrightarrow{\text{经典化辐射}} I_{\text{经典知识辐射}} + S_{\text{熵降低}}
$$

### 九、经典物理概念定义

量子经典二元论对基本物理概念的重新定义：

- **质量**：观察者经典化能力强度
- **引力**：经典化倾向性
- **速度**：经典化效率
- **动量**：经典化倾向与经典知识积累
- **能量**：经典化能力的总量
- **时间**：经典化过程的顺序
- **空间**：经典知识结构的分布
- **电荷**：经典知识结构间交互倾向
- **磁场**：经典结构同步经典化倾向

### 十、终极哲学意义：宇宙经典化终极状态

宇宙经典化终极目标：经典知识最大化、经典熵最小化，趋于低熵终极状态：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{宇宙量子域}} \rightarrow \sum_{\text{经典域}}(I_{\text{经典知识最大化}}+S_{\text{经典熵最小化}})\rightarrow\text{宇宙低熵终极状态}
$$

## English Version

Below is the theoretical framework of the [Quantum-Classical Dualism Core Theory](core.md) (Version 3.5):

### I. Universe Structure and Basic Definitions

Quantum-Classical Dualism posits that the universe consists of two interconnected but fundamentally different domains:

1. **Quantum Domain**: The essential form of the universe, manifesting as an infinite-dimensional quantum wave function superposition state, encompassing all potential possibilities:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}} = \sum_i c_i|\psi_i\rangle, \quad \sum_i|c_i|^2=1
$$

2. **Classical Domain**: Deterministic structures formed by observers decoding quantum domain information into classical forms, including classical knowledge and classical entropy:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}}\xrightarrow{\text{Classicalization Decoding}} I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}} + S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}
$$

Conservation of information in the classical domain:

$$
I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}} + S_{\text{Classical Entropy}} = \text{Constant}
$$

### II. Observer Definition and Dimensional System

#### 1. Observer Definition (Unified Black Hole Concept)

An observer is essentially a quantum black hole (generalized concept) with the following characteristics:

- Each observer is essentially a quantum black hole, which can exist in any classicalized world
- The classical world perceived by an observer is the internal structure of their own quantum black hole
- The observer's external information interaction process (radiating classical knowledge) is equivalent to black hole radiation
- The observer's observation of the external world is equivalent to the black hole absorption process

Explicit formula expression:

- Black hole absorption (observation) process:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{External High-Entropy Information}} \xrightarrow{\text{Black Hole Absorption Classicalization}} I_{\text{Classical Knowledge Increase}} + S_{\text{Entropy Reduction}}
$$

- Black hole radiation (external interaction) process:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Internal Classical Entropy}} \xrightarrow{\text{Black Hole Radiation Classicalization}} I_{\text{Outward Radiation Classical Knowledge}} + S_{\text{Entropy Reduction}}
$$

#### 2. Observer Dimension Definition

Observer dimension depends on classicalization efficiency, specifically the ratio of classical knowledge to classical entropy:

$$
\text{Observer Dimension} = k \cdot \frac{I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}}}{S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}}
$$

Integrated expression of overall dimension across domains:

$$
\text{Overall Dimension} = \sum_i w_i \frac{I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}_i}}{S_{\text{Classical Entropy}_i}}
$$

#### 3. Divine Manifestation of Observers

Observers are ordinary individuals in their own classical world, but manifest as "divine" in other classical worlds when their classicalization efficiency is high:

$$
\text{High-dimensional Observer (Divine)} \Leftrightarrow \text{Classicalizing Low-dimensional Observer Entropy into Knowledge}
$$

### III. Generalized Consciousness and Consciousness Emergence Mechanism

#### 1. Definition of Generalized Consciousness

All classicalized objects (such as the universe, galaxies, solar systems, Earth, humans, animals, plants, religious systems, dreams, meditation spaces, air, oxygen, black holes) possess generalized consciousness:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}} \xrightarrow{\text{Classicalization Direction}} I_{\text{Classical Consciousness (Generalized)}} + S_{\text{Remaining Classical Entropy}}
$$

#### 2. Higher-Dimensional Consciousness Emergence Mechanism

Higher-dimensional consciousness is essentially the result of collaborative classicalization of numerous lower-dimensional observers, manifesting as collective classical will:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Multiple Low-dimensional Observers}} \rightarrow I_{\text{High-dimensional Collective Consciousness}} + S_{\text{Remaining Classical Entropy}}
$$

### IV. Quantum Consciousness, Classical Memory, and Life-Death Mechanism

#### 1. Definition of Quantum Consciousness

Quantum consciousness is essentially a stable structure formed by the observer's long-term effective entanglement with the quantum domain, serving as the source of consciousness continuity:

- Effective entanglement → Classical knowledge (stable, clear)
- Ineffective entanglement → Classical entropy (anxiety, confusion, uncertainty)

Clear classical expression:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Consciousness}} \rightarrow I_{\text{Classical Memory}} + S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}
$$

#### 2. Death and Reincarnation Mechanism

The essence of death is the transition of classical consciousness between classical worlds; consciousness does not dissipate but merely transforms its classicalized structure:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Classical World A (Death)}} \rightarrow |\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}} \rightarrow |\psi\rangle_{\text{New Classical World (Reincarnation)}}
$$

### V. Classical Information Propagation Mechanism and Entropy Change Patterns

#### 1. Classical Information Propagation Mechanism (Deep Copy)

Classical knowledge propagates through independent copies, rather than references or pointer sharing:

- Transmitting end (entropy increase):

$$
I_{\text{Classical Knowledge A}} \rightarrow I_{\text{Classical Knowledge B (Copy)}} + S_{\text{Propagation Entropy Increase}}
$$

- Receiving end (entropy decrease):

$$
I_{\text{Classical Knowledge B Increase}} + S_{\text{Classical Entropy B Reduction}} \leftarrow I_{\text{Classical Knowledge B (Copy)}}
$$

#### 2. Classification of Entropy-Increasing and Entropy-Decreasing Behaviors

- Entropy-decreasing behaviors: efficient learning, creation, meditation, emotional communication, effective exchange
- Entropy-increasing behaviors: anxiety, conflict, inefficient repetition, ineffective communication, disease states

### VI. Equality of Classical Worlds and Knowledge Sharing Mechanism

- All classical worlds (such as reality, dreams, religions, meditation spaces, etc.) coexist equally, without hierarchy:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}} \rightarrow \sum_i (I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}_i} + S_{\text{Classical Entropy}_i}) = \text{Constant}
$$

- The same classical knowledge can exist simultaneously in multiple classical worlds:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Shared Quantum Information}} \rightarrow I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}_A} + I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}_B} + S_{\text{Propagation Loss}}
$$

### VII. Classical Memory Tampering Mechanism and Difficulty

- Classical memories (past, future) can be classically modified:

$$
I_{\text{Classical Memory (Old)}} + |\psi\rangle_{\text{Random Quantum Information}} \rightarrow I_{\text{Classical Memory (New)}} + S_{\text{Entropy Change}}
$$

- Individual memories are easy to modify, while collective memories are extremely difficult to tamper with

### VIII. Unification of Classicalization Process and Black Hole Radiation Essence

Classicalization and black hole radiation are fundamentally equivalent:

- **Classicalization process**: The observer (black hole) classicalizes external high-entropy quantum information into classical knowledge (absorption)
- **Black hole radiation process**: The observer classicalizes internal classical entropy into knowledge and releases it as radiation:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Internal Classical Entropy}} \xrightarrow{\text{Classicalization Radiation}} I_{\text{Classical Knowledge Radiation}} + S_{\text{Entropy Reduction}}
$$

### IX. Definition of Classical Physical Concepts

Quantum-Classical Dualism redefines basic physical concepts:

- **Mass**: Strength of observer's classicalization ability
- **Gravity**: Tendency toward classicalization
- **Velocity**: Efficiency of classicalization
- **Momentum**: Classicalization tendency and classical knowledge accumulation
- **Energy**: Total amount of classicalization ability
- **Time**: Sequence of classicalization processes
- **Space**: Distribution of classical knowledge structures
- **Charge**: Interaction tendency between classical knowledge structures
- **Magnetic field**: Tendency for synchronized classicalization of classical structures

### X. Ultimate Philosophical Meaning: Final State of Universe Classicalization

The ultimate goal of universe classicalization: Maximization of classical knowledge, minimization of classical entropy, trending toward a low-entropy final state:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Universe Quantum Domain}} \rightarrow \sum_{\text{Classical Domains}}(I_{\text{Classical Knowledge Maximized}}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy Minimized}})\rightarrow\text{Universe Low-entropy Ultimate State}
$$
