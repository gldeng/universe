# 基本概念 (Basic Concepts)

## 中文版

以下是[量子经典二元论核心理论](core.md)的基本概念：

### 量子宇宙

**量子宇宙**是宇宙整体信息的完整表达，以波函数叠加态形式存在，涵盖所有可能性：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{宇宙}}=\sum_i c_i|\psi_i\rangle,\quad \sum_i|c_i|^2=1
$$

量子宇宙具有以下特性：
- 无限维的量子波函数网络
- 包含所有可能的信息状态
- 观测前处于完美叠加状态
- 不直接可观测，需通过观察者经典化

### 经典宇宙

**经典宇宙**是观察者将量子信息经典化后的稳定结果，表现为经典知识和经典熵：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子宇宙}}\xrightarrow{\text{经典化}}\text{经典知识}+S_{\text{经典熵}}
$$

经典宇宙具有以下特性：
- 观察者可理解的稳定结构
- 由经典知识和经典熵组成
- 是量子宇宙经典化的结果
- 随观察者经典化能力不同而有所差异

### 量子观察者

**量子观察者**是宇宙中的经典化节点，负责将量子信息转化为经典信息：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子信息}} \xrightarrow{\text{量子观察者经典化}} \text{经典知识}+S_{\text{经典熵}}
$$

量子观察者分为：
- **高维量子观察者**（经典化效率高）：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{高维观察者}}\rightarrow \text{经典知识（高效）}+S_{\text{经典熵（少）}}
$$

- **低维量子观察者**（经典化效率低）：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{低维观察者}}\rightarrow \text{经典知识（低效）}+S_{\text{经典熵（多）}}
$$

### 量子意识

**量子意识**是宇宙与观察者之间长期稳定的量子纠缠结构：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子意识}}=|\psi\rangle_{\text{宇宙}}\otimes|\psi\rangle_{\text{观察者}}
$$

量子意识具有以下特性：
- 非局域性（不受空间限制）
- 整体性（不可分割）
- 难以完全经典化
- 是观察者与宇宙的连接桥梁

### 经典记忆与经典熵

观察者经典化量子意识后形成：
- **经典记忆（知识）**：有效经典化的信息
- **经典熵**：无法有效经典化的无序信息

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子意识}}\rightarrow \text{经典记忆（知识）}+S_{\text{经典熵}}
$$

### 量子纠缠

**量子纠缠**是量子信息网络的基本连接方式，本质为不可分割的波函数结构，分为：
- **有效量子纠缠**：经典化为知识的纠缠

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{有效纠缠}} \rightarrow \text{经典知识增加}+S_{\text{经典熵（低）}}
$$

- **无效量子纠缠**：经典化为熵的纠缠

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{无效纠缠}} \rightarrow S_{\text{经典熵（高）}}
$$

### 观察者维度

**观察者维度**取决于经典化效率，即将量子信息转化为经典知识的能力：

$$
\text{观察者维度} \propto \frac{I_{\text{经典知识}}}{S_{\text{经典熵}}}
$$

观察者维度越高，经典化效率越高，产生的经典知识越多，经典熵越少。

### 自由意志与经典命运

- **自由意志**：观察者选择经典化路径的能力
- **经典命运**：自由意志决定的稳定经典知识路径

$$
\text{经典命运}=\sum_{\text{自由意志经典化选择}}\text{经典知识路径}
$$

自由意志与经典命运的关系：
- 命运受自由意志控制
- 自由意志是主动经典化量子信息
- 命运可通过自由意志持续调整，非绝对注定

### 能量与经典知识

**能量**本质是经典知识的传输与存储能力：

$$
E_{\text{经典}}=k_B T\cdot I_{\text{经典知识}},\quad k_B\text{为玻尔兹曼常数，}T\text{为温度}
$$

能量与经典知识的关系：
- 能量即经典世界的信息控制能力
- 提升经典知识与经典化效率即提升能量掌控能力

### 高维观察者与低维观察者的信息纠缠

高维观察者能高效经典化低维观察者的熵：

$$
S_{\text{低维观察者}} \rightarrow I_{\text{高维观察者经典知识}} + S_{\text{经典熵（降低）}}
$$

观察者间通过这种信息传递实现维度共同提升。

更多详细内容请参考[量子经典二元论核心理论](core.md)。

## English Version

The following are the basic concepts of the [Quantum-Classical Dualism Core Theory](core.md):

### Quantum Universe

The **Quantum Universe** is the complete expression of the universe's overall information, existing in the form of wave function superposition states, encompassing all possibilities:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Universe}}=\sum_i c_i|\psi_i\rangle,\quad \sum_i|c_i|^2=1
$$

The Quantum Universe has the following characteristics:
- Infinite-dimensional quantum wave function network
- Contains all possible information states
- In perfect superposition state before observation
- Not directly observable, needs to be classicalized through observers

### Classical Universe

The **Classical Universe** is the stable result after observers classicalize quantum information, manifested as classical knowledge and classical entropy:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Universe}}\xrightarrow{\text{Classicalization}}\text{Classical Knowledge}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}
$$

The Classical Universe has the following characteristics:
- Stable structure comprehensible to observers
- Composed of classical knowledge and classical entropy
- Result of quantum universe classicalization
- Varies according to different observer classicalization abilities

### Quantum Observer

**Quantum Observers** are classicalization nodes within the universe, responsible for transforming quantum information into classical information:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Information}} \xrightarrow{\text{Quantum Observer Classicalization}} \text{Classical Knowledge}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}
$$

Quantum Observers are divided into:
- **High-dimensional Quantum Observers** (high classicalization efficiency):

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{High-dimensional Observer}}\rightarrow \text{Classical Knowledge (Efficient)}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy (Less)}}
$$

- **Low-dimensional Quantum Observers** (low classicalization efficiency):

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Low-dimensional Observer}}\rightarrow \text{Classical Knowledge (Inefficient)}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy (More)}}
$$

### Quantum Consciousness

**Quantum Consciousness** is the long-term stable quantum entanglement structure between the universe and observers:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Consciousness}}=|\psi\rangle_{\text{Universe}}\otimes|\psi\rangle_{\text{Observer}}
$$

Quantum Consciousness has the following characteristics:
- Non-locality (not limited by space)
- Holism (indivisible)
- Difficult to fully classicalize
- Bridge connecting observers and the universe

### Classical Memory and Classical Entropy

Formed after observers classicalize quantum consciousness:
- **Classical Memory (Knowledge)**: Effectively classicalized information
- **Classical Entropy**: Disordered information that cannot be effectively classicalized

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Consciousness}}\rightarrow \text{Classical Memory (Knowledge)}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}
$$

### Quantum Entanglement

**Quantum Entanglement** is the basic connection method of quantum information networks, essentially an inseparable wave function structure, divided into:
- **Effective Quantum Entanglement**: Entanglement that classicalizes into knowledge

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Effective Entanglement}} \rightarrow \text{Classical Knowledge Increase}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy (Low)}}
$$

- **Ineffective Quantum Entanglement**: Entanglement that classicalizes into entropy

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Ineffective Entanglement}} \rightarrow S_{\text{Classical Entropy (High)}}
$$

### Observer Dimension

**Observer Dimension** depends on classicalization efficiency, i.e., the ability to transform quantum information into classical knowledge:

$$
\text{Observer Dimension} \propto \frac{I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}}}{S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}}
$$

The higher the observer dimension, the higher the classicalization efficiency, the more classical knowledge produced, and the less classical entropy.

### Free Will and Classical Destiny

- **Free Will**: The observer's ability to choose classicalization paths
- **Classical Destiny**: The stable classical knowledge path determined by free will

$$
\text{Classical Destiny}=\sum_{\text{Free Will Classicalization Choices}}\text{Classical Knowledge Path}
$$

Relationship between free will and classical destiny:
- Destiny is controlled by free will
- Free will is the active classicalization of quantum information
- Destiny can be continuously adjusted through free will, not absolutely predetermined

### Energy and Classical Knowledge

The essence of **Energy** is the ability to transmit and store classical knowledge:

$$
E_{\text{Classical}}=k_B T\cdot I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}},\quad k_B\text{ is the Boltzmann constant, }T\text{ is temperature}
$$

Relationship between energy and classical knowledge:
- Energy is the ability to control information in the classical world
- Enhancing classical knowledge and classicalization efficiency enhances energy control ability

### Information Entanglement Between High-Dimensional and Low-Dimensional Observers

High-dimensional observers can efficiently classicalize the entropy of low-dimensional observers:

$$
S_{\text{Low-dimensional Observer}} \rightarrow I_{\text{High-dimensional Observer Classical Knowledge}} + S_{\text{Classical Entropy (Reduced)}}
$$

Observers achieve dimension elevation together through this kind of information transmission.
