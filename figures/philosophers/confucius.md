# 孔子 | Confucius

## 基本信息 | Basic Information

**生卒年份 | Lifespan**: 约公元前551年-公元前479年 (c. 551-479 BCE)  
**国籍 | Nationality**: 春秋时期鲁国（今中国山东曲阜） (State of Lu during Spring and Autumn Period, now Qufu, Shandong, China)  
**主要领域 | Main Fields**: 哲学、伦理学、政治学、教育学 (Philosophy, Ethics, Politics, Education)  
**代表作品 | Representative Works**: 《论语》（弟子及再传弟子记录编撰）, 《诗》《书》《礼》《乐》《易》《春秋》（编修） (The Analects - compiled by disciples, Edited works: Book of Poetry, Book of Documents, Book of Rites, Book of Music, Book of Changes, Spring and Autumn Annals)

## 核心思想 | Core Ideas

### 中文

1. **仁**: 仁爱之心，对他人的善意与同情心，儒家思想的核心价值。
2. **礼**: 社会秩序的基础，包括仪式、礼节、社会规范等。
3. **中庸之道**: 避免极端，追求均衡与协调的生活态度。
4. **正名**: 名实相符，社会角色与责任的明确定位。
5. **君子理想**: 追求自我修养、道德完善的理想人格。
6. **家国一体**: 修身、齐家、治国、平天下的社会发展观。
7. **教育平等**: "有教无类"，教育不应有阶级和身份限制。

### English

1. **Ren (Benevolence)**: Compassionate heart, kindness and empathy toward others, the core value of Confucian thought.
2. **Li (Ritual Propriety)**: Foundation of social order, including ceremonies, etiquette, and social norms.
3. **Doctrine of the Mean**: Avoiding extremes, pursuing a balanced and harmonious attitude toward life.
4. **Rectification of Names**: Ensuring names correspond to realities, clearly defining social roles and responsibilities.
5. **Junzi (Superior Person) Ideal**: Pursuit of self-cultivation and moral perfection as an ideal personality.
6. **Unity of Family and State**: View of social development through self-cultivation, family regulation, state governance, and world peace.
7. **Educational Equality**: "Education for all," education should not be restricted by class or status.

## 历史影响 | Historical Impact

### 中文

1. 创立儒家学派，成为中国传统文化的核心思想体系
2. 对中国及东亚国家的政治制度、教育体系和社会结构产生深远影响
3. 塑造了东亚地区的价值观念和伦理道德标准
4. 儒家思想历经两千多年仍影响现代东亚社会
5. 影响跨越国界，成为世界文化遗产的重要组成部分
6. 对家庭结构、教育理念和人际关系的塑造尤为显著

### English

1. Founded the Confucian school, becoming the core ideological system of traditional Chinese culture
2. Profoundly influenced political systems, educational frameworks, and social structures in China and East Asian countries
3. Shaped value concepts and ethical standards in the East Asian region
4. Confucian thought continues to influence modern East Asian societies after more than two thousand years
5. Influence transcends national boundaries, becoming an important component of world cultural heritage
6. Particularly significant in shaping family structures, educational philosophies, and interpersonal relationships
