# 沃尔夫冈·阿玛多伊斯·莫扎特 | Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

## 基本信息 | Basic Information

**生卒年份 | Lifespan**: 1756年-1791年 (1756-1791)  
**国籍 | Nationality**: 奥地利 (Austria)  
**主要领域 | Main Fields**: 古典音乐作曲、钢琴、小提琴、歌剧 (Classical Music Composition, Piano, Violin, Opera)  
**代表作品 | Representative Works**: 《费加罗的婚礼》、《唐璜》、《魔笛》、《安魂曲》、第40号交响曲、《小夜曲》、《土耳其进行曲》 (The Marriage of Figaro, Don Giovanni, The Magic Flute, Requiem, Symphony No. 40, Eine kleine Nachtmusik, Turkish March)

## 核心思想 | Core Ideas

### 中文

1. **结构与表达平衡**: 在严格的古典音乐形式内实现情感表达的完美平衡，展现了形式约束与创造自由的辩证统一。
2. **自然优雅原则**: 莫扎特的音乐展现出看似简单、实则深邃的自然优雅，他相信最深刻的美源于清晰与平衡。
3. **情感的普遍性**: 通过音乐语言表达人类共通的情感体验，超越语言文化的限制，实现情感的普遍表达。
4. **对位与和声融合**: 独特地融合巴洛克对位法与新兴的古典和声，创造出层次丰富而又清晰透明的音乐织体。
5. **戏剧化音乐叙事**: 将音乐视为一种叙事形式，能够表达复杂的戏剧性和人物心理，尤其体现在其歌剧创作中。
6. **主题变形技巧**: 通过主题的变形、发展和重组，展示简单音乐素材内蕴含的无限可能性。
7. **艺术的游戏性**: 在严肃艺术创作中保持游戏般的轻盈与幽默，反映出艺术创作源于纯粹的创造喜悦。

### English

1. **Balance of Structure and Expression**: Achieving perfect balance between emotional expression within strict classical forms, demonstrating the dialectical unity of formal constraints and creative freedom.
2. **Principle of Natural Elegance**: Mozart's music exhibits a seemingly simple yet profound natural elegance, reflecting his belief that the deepest beauty arises from clarity and balance.
3. **Universality of Emotion**: Expressing universally shared human emotional experiences through musical language, transcending limitations of verbal language and culture to achieve universal emotional expression.
4. **Integration of Counterpoint and Harmony**: Uniquely fusing Baroque counterpoint with emerging classical harmony, creating musical textures that are both richly layered and transparently clear.
5. **Dramatic Musical Narrative**: Viewing music as a narrative form capable of expressing complex dramatic situations and psychological states, particularly evident in his operatic works.
6. **Thematic Transformation Technique**: Demonstrating the infinite possibilities contained within simple musical materials through transformation, development, and recombination of themes.
7. **Playfulness in Art**: Maintaining a game-like lightness and humor in serious artistic creation, reflecting the view that artistic creation springs from pure creative joy.
