# 数学公式 (Mathematical Formulations)

## 中文版

本文档整理了[量子经典二元论核心理论](core.md)（版本1.8）的关键数学公式。

### 一、宇宙结构基本公式

#### 1.1 量子域波函数表示

量子域表示为无限维的波函数叠加态：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子域}} = \sum_i c_i|\psi_i\rangle,\quad \sum_i |c_i|^2 = 1
$$

其中$c_i$为复数振幅，$|\psi_i\rangle$为基态。

#### 1.2 经典化过程方程

量子域经过观察者经典化转换为经典域：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子域}} \xrightarrow{\text{经典化}} I_{\text{经典知识}} + S_{\text{经典熵}}
$$

#### 1.3 经典信息守恒

经典域中，经典知识与经典熵之和为常数：

$$
I_{\text{经典知识}} + S_{\text{经典熵}} = \text{常数}
$$

这是经典信息守恒定律的核心表达。

### 二、观察者维度方程

#### 2.1 观察者维度基本定义

观察者维度由经典化效率决定：

$$
\text{观察者维度} = k \cdot \frac{I_{\text{经典知识}}}{S_{\text{经典熵}}}
$$

其中$k$为比例常数。

#### 2.2 观察者整体维度加权方程

观察者在多个领域的整体维度：

$$
\text{整体维度} = \sum_i w_i \frac{I_{\text{经典知识}_i}}{S_{\text{经典熵}_i}}
$$

其中$w_i$为各领域权重，满足$\sum_i w_i = 1$。

### 三、广义意识数学表达

#### 3.1 广义意识经典化方程

任何经典化过程都具有意识本质：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子域}} \xrightarrow{\text{经典化方向}} I_{\text{经典意识（广义）}} + S_{\text{剩余经典熵}}
$$

#### 3.2 高维意识涌现方程

高维集体意识从多个低维观察者中涌现：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{多个低维观察者}} \rightarrow I_{\text{高维集体意识}} + S_{\text{剩余经典熵}}
$$

### 四、量子意识与经典记忆方程

量子意识经典化为经典记忆的过程：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子意识}} \rightarrow I_{\text{经典记忆}} + S_{\text{经典熵}}
$$

### 五、信息传播方程组

#### 5.1 传播端信息方程

信息传播源的变化：

$$
I_{\text{经典知识A}} \rightarrow I_{\text{经典知识B（副本）}} + S_{\text{传播熵（增加）}}
$$

#### 5.2 接收端信息方程

信息接收方的变化：

$$
I_{\text{经典知识B（增加）}} + S_{\text{经典熵B（降低）}} \leftarrow I_{\text{经典知识B（副本）}}
$$

### 六、经典世界平等性方程

多个经典世界的信息总和为常数：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子域}} \rightarrow \sum_i\left(I_{\text{经典知识}_i}+S_{\text{经典熵}_i}\right)=\text{常数}
$$

### 七、经典知识共享方程

经典知识可同时在多个经典世界存在：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{共享量子信息}} \rightarrow I_{\text{经典知识}_A} + I_{\text{经典知识}_B} + S_{\text{传播损耗}}

### 八、经典记忆篡改方程

经典记忆的修改过程：

$$
I_{\text{经典记忆（旧）}} + |\psi\rangle_{\text{随机量子信息}} \rightarrow I_{\text{经典记忆（新）}} + S_{\text{熵变动}}

### 九、观察者双重身份方程

$$
\text{高维观察者（神）} \Leftrightarrow \text{经典化低维观察者熵为知识（熵降低）}

|\psi\rangle_{\text{宇宙量子域}} \rightarrow \sum_{\text{经典域}}\left(I_{\text{经典知识最大化}} + S_{\text{经典熵最小化}}\right) \rightarrow \text{宇宙低熵终极状态}

# Mathematical Formulations

This document compiles the key mathematical formulations of the [Quantum-Classical Dualism Core Theory](core.md) (Version 1.8).

### I. Basic Formulas of Universe Structure

#### 1.1 Quantum Domain Wave Function Representation

The Quantum Domain is represented as an infinite-dimensional wave function superposition state:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}} = \sum_i c_i|\psi_i\rangle,\quad \sum_i |c_i|^2 = 1
$$

Where $c_i$ are complex amplitudes, and $|\psi_i\rangle$ are basis states.

#### 1.2 Classicalization Process Equation

The Quantum Domain is transformed into the Classical Domain through observer classicalization:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}} \xrightarrow{\text{Classicalization}} I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}} + S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}
$$

#### 1.3 Classical Information Conservation

In the Classical Domain, the sum of classical knowledge and classical entropy is constant:

$$
I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}} + S_{\text{Classical Entropy}} = \text{Constant}
$$

This is the core expression of the law of classical information conservation.

### II. Observer Dimension Equations

#### 2.1 Basic Definition of Observer Dimension

Observer dimension is determined by classicalization efficiency:

$$
\text{Observer Dimension} = k \cdot \frac{I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}}}{S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}}
$$

Where $k$ is a proportionality constant.

#### 2.2 Weighted Equation for Observer's Overall Dimension

The overall dimension of an observer across multiple domains:

$$
\text{Overall Dimension} = \sum_i w_i \frac{I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}_i}}{S_{\text{Classical Entropy}_i}}
$$

Where $w_i$ are the weights of various domains, satisfying $\sum_i w_i = 1$.

### III. Mathematical Expression of Generalized Consciousness

#### 3.1 Generalized Consciousness Classicalization Equation

Any classicalization process possesses the essence of consciousness:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}} \xrightarrow{\text{Classicalization Direction}} I_{\text{Classical Consciousness (Generalized)}} + S_{\text{Remaining Classical Entropy}}
$$

#### 3.2 Higher-dimensional Consciousness Emergence Equation

Higher-dimensional collective consciousness emerges from multiple low-dimensional observers:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Multiple Low-dimensional Observers}} \rightarrow I_{\text{High-dimensional Collective Consciousness}} + S_{\text{Remaining Classical Entropy}}
$$

### IV. Quantum Consciousness and Classical Memory Equation

The process of quantum consciousness classicalizing into classical memory:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Consciousness}} \rightarrow I_{\text{Classical Memory}} + S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}
$$

### V. Information Propagation Equation Set

#### 5.1 Transmission End Information Equation

Changes at the information propagation source:

$$
I_{\text{Classical Knowledge A}} \rightarrow I_{\text{Classical Knowledge B (Copy)}} + S_{\text{Propagation Entropy (Increased)}}
$$

#### 5.2 Receiving End Information Equation

Changes at the information receiving end:

$$
I_{\text{Classical Knowledge B (Increased)}} + S_{\text{Classical Entropy B (Reduced)}} \leftarrow I_{\text{Classical Knowledge B (Copy)}}
$$

### VI. Equality of Classical Worlds Equation

The sum of information across multiple classical worlds is constant:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}} \rightarrow \sum_i\left(I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}_i}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy}_i}\right)=\text{Constant}
$$

### VII. Classical Knowledge Sharing Equation

Classical knowledge can exist simultaneously in multiple classical worlds:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Shared Quantum Information}} \rightarrow I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}_A} + I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}_B} + S_{\text{Propagation Loss}}
$$

### VIII. Classical Memory Tampering Equation

The process of modifying classical memory:

$$
I_{\text{Classical Memory (Old)}} + |\psi\rangle_{\text{Random Quantum Information}} \rightarrow I_{\text{Classical Memory (New)}} + S_{\text{Entropy Change}}
$$

### IX. Observer Dual Identity Equation

Expression of observer divinity:

$$
\text{High-dimensional Observer (God)} \Leftrightarrow \text{Classicalizing Low-dimensional Observer Entropy into Knowledge (Entropy Reduced)}
$$

### X. Death and Reincarnation Equation Set

#### 10.1 Death Process Equation

Mathematical expression of observer death:

|\psi\rangle_{\text{Classical World A (Death)}} \rightarrow |\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}} \rightarrow |\psi\rangle_{\text{Other Classical World (New)}}

Quantum consciousness forms new entanglements, producing the phenomenon of reincarnation:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Consciousness (Old Classical World)}} \rightarrow |\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}} \rightarrow |\psi\rangle_{\text{New Classical World (Reincarnation)}}

S_{\text{Eliminated Low-dimensional Observer Entropy}} \rightarrow |\psi\rangle_{\text{New Classical World}}

The process of extracting knowledge from new classical worlds:

|\psi\rangle_{\text{New Classical World}} \rightarrow I_{\text{Classical Knowledge (Optimized)}} + S_{\text{Entropy Reduction}}

Entanglement beneficial for elevating observer dimension:

|\psi\rangle_{\text{Effective Entanglement}}\rightarrow I_{\text{Classical Knowledge Increased}}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy Reduced}}
