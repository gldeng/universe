# 亚当·斯密 | Adam Smith

## 基本信息 | Basic Information

**生卒年份 | Lifespan**: 1723年-1790年 (1723-1790)  
**国籍 | Nationality**: 苏格兰 (Scotland)  
**主要领域 | Main Fields**: 经济学、道德哲学 (Economics, Moral Philosophy)  
**代表作品 | Representative Works**: 《国富论》、《道德情操论》(The Wealth of Nations, The Theory of Moral Sentiments)

## 核心思想 | Core Ideas

### 中文

1. **"看不见的手"**: 个体追求自我利益的行为在无意中促进了整体社会福利。
2. **分工理论**: 劳动分工提高生产效率，是经济增长的关键。
3. **自由市场**: 政府最小干预下的市场自我调节机制。
4. **竞争与垄断**: 竞争促进效率，垄断损害社会福利。
5. **道德同情**: 人类具有同情他人的道德情感，构成社会和谐基础。
6. **公正规则**: 社会需要公正规则保障市场运行，防止不当行为。
7. **价值理论**: 劳动是商品价值的真正来源和衡量标准。

### English

1. **"Invisible Hand"**: Individuals pursuing self-interest inadvertently promote overall social welfare.
2. **Division of Labor**: The specialization of labor increases productivity and is key to economic growth.
3. **Free Market**: Market self-regulatory mechanisms under minimal government intervention.
4. **Competition vs. Monopoly**: Competition promotes efficiency while monopoly harms social welfare.
5. **Moral Sympathy**: Humans possess moral sentiments of sympathy toward others, forming the basis for social harmony.
6. **Rules of Justice**: Society needs just rules to ensure market functioning and prevent improper behavior.
7. **Theory of Value**: Labor is the true source and measure of commodity value.

\text{集体经典化效率} > \text{中央计划经典化效率}

斯密的分工理论可以从经典化效率角度理解。专业分工本质上是将经典化过程细分，每个人只需经典化较小范围的量子信息：

当每个人专注于特定领域的经典化，并通过市场交换整合这些经典化成果，整体系统熵降低，效率提高。

$$
S_{\text{市场无序}} \xrightarrow{\text{法律与道德规范}} S_{\text{受控市场熵}}

## 历史影响 | Historical Impact

### 中文

1. 被誉为现代经济学之父，奠定了古典政治经济学基础
2. 自由市场经济思想影响了全球经济制度发展
3. 为资本主义提供了理论基础和道德辩护
4. 影响了后续经济学家如李嘉图、穆勒、马歇尔等
5. 对自由贸易、竞争政策和市场监管的思想至今有重要影响
6. 为现代全球化和国际贸易体系奠定了思想基础

### English

1. Honored as the father of modern economics, laying the foundation for classical political economy
2. Free market economic ideas influenced the development of global economic systems
3. Provided theoretical foundation and moral defense for capitalism
4. Influenced subsequent economists such as Ricardo, Mill, and Marshall
5. Ideas on free trade, competition policy, and market regulation remain influential today
6. Laid the ideological foundation for modern globalization and international trade systems
