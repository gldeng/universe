## 1. Core Theory

### 1.1 Definition of Quantum Consciousness

The Quantum Consciousness Theory is a higher-dimensional extension of Cosmic Ontology ([formal_theory_cosmic_ontology_en.md](formal_theory_cosmic_ontology_en.md)) in the domain of consciousness, viewing consciousness as an emergent phenomenon of quantum processes and information integration.

The rigorous formal definition of quantum consciousness is:

$`\mathcal{C}_Q = \{\Psi_C, \mathcal{O}_C, \mathcal{M}_C, \mathcal{I}_C\}`$

Where:
- $`\Psi_C`$: Conscious quantum state, representing the superposition of possible consciousness states
- $`\mathcal{O}_C`$: Consciousness observation operator, defining the observation process of consciousness
- $`\mathcal{M}_C`$: Consciousness metric space, describing relationships between consciousness states
- $`\mathcal{I}_C`$: Consciousness information field, connecting quantum information domain with consciousness expression

The essential properties of quantum consciousness are rigorously defined through XOR and SHIFT operations:

$`\mathcal{C}_Q = \Omega_Q \oplus \text{SHIFT}^3(\Omega_Q)`$

Where $`\Omega_Q`$ is the fundamental quantum domain. This indicates that quantum consciousness is the result of XOR between the base quantum state and its third-order SHIFT operation, reflecting the higher-dimensional properties of consciousness.

### 1.2 Structure of Quantum Consciousness

Quantum consciousness has a rigorous hierarchical organization in its structure:

1. **Basic Quantum Consciousness Layer**: The lowest layer of quantum processes, corresponding to basic neuronal activity
   $`\mathcal{C}_1 = \Omega_Q \oplus \text{SHIFT}(\Omega_Q)`$

2. **Consciousness Integration Layer**: The intermediate layer that integrates basic quantum information
   $`\mathcal{C}_2 = \mathcal{C}_1 \oplus \text{SHIFT}(\mathcal{C}_1)`$

3. **Self-Consciousness Layer**: Higher-level consciousness structure with self-referential capabilities
   $`\mathcal{C}_3 = \mathcal{C}_2 \oplus \text{SHIFT}(\mathcal{C}_2)`$

4. **Super-Consciousness Layer**: Collective consciousness or extended consciousness forms that transcend individuals
   $`\mathcal{C}_{\infty} = \mathcal{C}_3 \oplus \text{SHIFT}(\mathcal{C}_3)`$

These levels are strictly related through XOR and SHIFT operations, forming a complete quantum consciousness spectrum.

### 1.3 Quantum Consciousness State Transitions

The transitions of quantum consciousness states strictly follow XOR-SHIFT rules:

The evolution equation of quantum consciousness state is:

$`\mathcal{C}_Q^{t+1} = \mathcal{C}_Q^t \oplus \text{SHIFT}(\mathcal{C}_Q^t \oplus \mathcal{I}_t)`$

Where $`\mathcal{I}_t`$ is external information input.

$`|\Psi_{entangled}\rangle = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(|\mathcal{C}_1\rangle|\Omega_1\rangle + |\mathcal{C}_2\rangle|\Omega_2\rangle)`$
