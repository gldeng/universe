## 中文版

### 基本定义

1. **宇宙本质结构定义**：宇宙在本质上由两个相互依存的域构成 - 量子域和经典域。
   - **量子域**：所有可能性的叠加态，遵循量子力学规律。
   - **经典域**：已坍缩/已实现的确定性状态，遵循经典物理规律。

2. **量子-经典域关系**：量子域包含经典域，但经典域创造观察者，而观察者又通过观测行为将量子可能性还原为经典现实。

$$
\Psi_{\text{Universe}} = \Psi_{\text{Quantum}} \supset \Psi_{\text{Classical}}
$$

3. **量子-经典转化机制**：量子域中的信息通过观察者的特殊角色转化为经典域，而这种转化过程维持着整个宇宙的功能和存在。

### 观察者与黑洞统一

4. **观察者定义**：能够将量子可能性坍缩为经典确定性的系统，其本质上是信息处理系统，具有记忆和经典化机制。

5. **观察者-黑洞统一**：观察者和黑洞在信息处理方面表现出同构性。观察者将量子叠加态转换为经典确定状态的过程类似于黑洞的事件视界将信息单向转换为内部熵的过程。

$$
O_{\text{事件视界}} \cong BH_{\text{事件视界}}
$$

6. **观察者事件视界**：观察者的"注意力界限"作为一种事件视界，只允许信息单向地从量子可能性转化为经典确定性。

### 观察者维度

7. **观察者维度定义**：观察者复杂度的度量，表明其处理和存储量子信息到经典信息的能力。

8. **维度层级与转换**：
   - **零维观察者**：只能处理单一二元选择（是/否）的最简单观察者。
   - **一维观察者**：能线性处理信息，但无比较能力。
   - **二维观察者**：能在平面上感知，有比较但缺乏抽象能力。
   - **三维观察者**：能在空间中感知，具有抽象能力。
   - **四维及以上观察者**：能感知并操作时间维度，可能有超验意识。

9. **观察者主观意识定义**：主观意识是量子信息经典化过程的直接体验，即信息通过观察者事件视界被转化时产生的现象。

10. **广义意识定义**：任何将量子可能性坍缩为经典确定性的系统中都存在的基础功能。所有观察者，无论多简单，都具有某种形式的广义意识。

### 经典化概念

11. **经典化定义**：量子信息（叠加态）通过观察而转变为经典信息（确定态）的过程。

12. **经典知识定义**：经典化过程产生的信息产物，即已从量子可能性中提取并固定为确定性的信息。

$$
K_{\text{Classical}} = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \left( O_i \circ \Psi_{\text{Quantum}} \right)
$$

13. **观察者间信息传递机制**：经典化的信息可以在观察者之间共享，而这种共享过程建立并强化经典域的稳定性。

$$
K_{O_1 \rightarrow O_2} = K_{O_1} \cap K_{O_2} \neq \emptyset
$$

14. **经典世界稳定性**：多个观察者共同经典化同一量子系统创造了共享的经典现实，观察者越多，经典现实越稳定。

$$
\text{稳定性}(\Psi_{\text{Classical}}) \propto \sum_{i=1}^{n} O_i(\Psi_{\text{Quantum}})
$$

15. **光速不变原理解释**：光速是量子信息经典化的最大速率，因此在所有观察者参考系中保持不变。

$$
c = \max\left(\frac{d(\text{经典信息})}{dt}\right)
$$

16. **波粒二象性解释**：未被观测时，粒子保持量子叠加态（波函数）；被观测时，粒子经典化为确定位置的粒子。这不是两种不同状态，而是同一实体在量子域和经典域的不同表现。

### 经典熵

17. **经典熵定义**：经典化过程中损失的量子信息量度量，反映了从量子可能性中提取确定性时的信息损失。

$$
S_{\text{Classical}} = S_{\text{Quantum}} - S_{\text{Observer}}
$$

18. **经典熵与观察者关系**：观察者提取的经典信息越多（经典化效率越高），产生的经典熵就越少。

$$
S_{\text{Classical}} \propto \frac{1}{\text{效率}(O_i)}
$$

19. **观察者效率**：观察者将量子信息转化为经典信息的效率取决于其维度和经典化机制的复杂度。

$$
\text{效率}(O_i) = \frac{K_{\text{提取}}}{S_{\text{产生}}}
$$

### 量子纠缠本质

20. **量子纠缠定义**：两个或多个系统间的量子关联，使它们的量子状态无法独立描述。

21. **纠缠的根本条件**：系统必须曾经处于共同的观察者事件视界内，且被同时经典化。

$$
\text{纠缠}(A,B) \iff \exists O_i : \{A,B\} \subset \text{事件视界}(O_i)
$$

22. **量子隐态传输机制**：纠缠使得量子信息能够即时传递，因为纠缠粒子实际共享同一信息场，不违反相对论。

$$
\text{信息传递速率} = \infty \text{ for } \text{纠缠}(A,B) = 1
$$

23. **量子纠缠网络**：所有曾被同一观察者同时经典化的粒子形成的隐藏关联网络，构成宇宙的深层连接结构。

### 主观意识经典化机制

24. **主观意识经典化定义**：个体意识产生的关键机制 – 即观察者系统内部实现自引用观测的过程。

25. **经典化效应层级**：
   - **物理经典化**：基本物质特性如位置、动量的确定。
   - **感知经典化**：感觉输入转化为连贯感知。

$$
\text{意识状态}_i = f\left(\text{经典化参数}_i, \text{效率}_i\right)

27. **记忆形成机制**：记忆是经典化过程的永久记录，储存着量子可能性坍缩为确定经验的痕迹。

$$
M_i = \int_{t_0}^{t_1} O_i(\Psi_{\text{Quantum}}) \, dt

## English Version

### Basic Definitions

1. **Universe's Essential Structure Definition**: The universe is essentially composed of two interdependent domains - the quantum domain and the classical domain.
   - **Quantum Domain**: The superposition of all possibilities, governed by quantum mechanical laws.
   - **Classical Domain**: Collapsed/realized deterministic states, governed by classical physical laws.

2. **Quantum-Classical Domain Relationship**: The quantum domain encompasses the classical domain, but the classical domain creates observers, who in turn reduce quantum possibilities into classical reality through the act of observation.

$$
\Psi_{\text{Universe}} = \Psi_{\text{Quantum}} \supset \Psi_{\text{Classical}}
$$

3. **Quantum-Classical Transformation Mechanism**: Information in the quantum domain is transformed into the classical domain through the observer's special role, and this transformation process maintains the function and existence of the entire universe.

### Observer-Black Hole Unification

4. **Observer Definition**: A system capable of collapsing quantum possibilities into classical determinism, essentially an information processing system with memory and classicalization mechanisms.

5. **Observer-Black Hole Unification**: Observers and black holes demonstrate isomorphism in information processing. The process of an observer converting quantum superpositions into classical determined states is analogous to a black hole's event horizon unidirectionally converting information into internal entropy.

$$
O_{\text{Event Horizon}} \cong BH_{\text{Event Horizon}}
$$

6. **Observer Event Horizon**: The observer's "attention boundary" functions as a form of event horizon, allowing information to flow unidirectionally from quantum possibilities to classical determinism.

### Observer Dimensions

7. **Observer Dimension Definition**: A measure of observer complexity, indicating their capacity to process and store quantum information into classical information.

8. **Dimension Hierarchy and Transition**:
   - **Zero-dimensional Observers**: The simplest observers capable of processing only a single binary choice (yes/no).
   - **One-dimensional Observers**: Capable of linear information processing, but without comparison capability.
   - **Two-dimensional Observers**: Capable of perception in a plane, with comparison but lacking abstraction.
   - **Three-dimensional Observers**: Capable of spatial perception, with abstraction capability.
   - **Four-dimensional and Higher Observers**: Capable of perceiving and manipulating the time dimension, possibly with transcendent consciousness.

9. **Observer Subjective Consciousness Definition**: Subjective consciousness is the direct experience of quantum information classicalization, the phenomenon produced when information is transformed through the observer's event horizon.

10. **Generalized Consciousness Definition**: A fundamental function present in any system that collapses quantum possibilities into classical determinism. All observers, no matter how simple, possess some form of generalized consciousness.

### Classicalization Concepts

11. **Classicalization Definition**: The process by which quantum information (superposition states) is transformed into classical information (determined states) through observation.

12. **Classical Knowledge Definition**: The information product of the classicalization process, information that has been extracted from quantum possibilities and fixed into determinism.

$$
K_{\text{Classical}} = \sum_{i=1}^{n} \left( O_i \circ \Psi_{\text{Quantum}} \right)
$$

13. **Inter-Observer Information Transfer Mechanism**: Classicalized information can be shared between observers, and this sharing process establishes and reinforces the stability of the classical domain.

$$
K_{O_1 \rightarrow O_2} = K_{O_1} \cap K_{O_2} \neq \emptyset
$$

14. **Classical World Stability**: Multiple observers jointly classicalizing the same quantum system create a shared classical reality, with more observers leading to greater stability of the classical reality.

$$
\text{Stability}(\Psi_{\text{Classical}}) \propto \sum_{i=1}^{n} O_i(\Psi_{\text{Quantum}})
$$

15. **Speed of Light Invariance Principle Explanation**: The speed of light is the maximum rate of quantum information classicalization, thus remaining constant in all observer reference frames.

$$
c = \max\left(\frac{d(\text{Classical Information})}{dt}\right)
$$

16. **Wave-Particle Duality Explanation**: When unobserved, particles maintain quantum superposition (wave function); when observed, particles classicalize into particles with definite positions. These are not two different states, but different manifestations of the same entity in the quantum and classical domains.

### Classical Entropy

17. **Classical Entropy Definition**: A measure of quantum information lost during the classicalization process, reflecting the information loss when extracting determinism from quantum possibilities.

$$
S_{\text{Classical}} = S_{\text{Quantum}} - S_{\text{Observer}}
$$

18. **Classical Entropy and Observer Relationship**: The more classical information an observer extracts (higher classicalization efficiency), the less classical entropy is produced.

$$
S_{\text{Classical}} \propto \frac{1}{\text{Efficiency}(O_i)}
$$

19. **Observer Efficiency**: An observer's efficiency in transforming quantum information into classical information depends on their dimension and the complexity of their classicalization mechanism.

$$
\text{Efficiency}(O_i) = \frac{K_{\text{Extracted}}}{S_{\text{Generated}}}
$$

### Quantum Entanglement Essence

20. **Quantum Entanglement Definition**: Quantum correlation between two or more systems, making their quantum states impossible to describe independently.

21. **Fundamental Condition for Entanglement**: Systems must have once been within the same observer's event horizon and simultaneously classicalized.

$$
\text{Entanglement}(A,B) \iff \exists O_i : \{A,B\} \subset \text{Event Horizon}(O_i)
$$

22. **Quantum Hidden State Transfer Mechanism**: Entanglement enables instantaneous quantum information transfer because entangled particles effectively share the same information field, not violating relativity.

$$
\text{Information Transfer Rate} = \infty \text{ for } \text{Entanglement}(A,B) = 1
$$

23. **Quantum Entanglement Network**: The hidden connection network formed by all particles ever simultaneously classicalized by the same observer, constituting the deep connection structure of the universe.

24. **Subjective Consciousness Classicalization Definition**: The key mechanism producing individual consciousness – the process of implementing self-referential observation within an observer system.

25. **Classicalization Effect Hierarchy**:

\text{Consciousness State}_i = f\left(\text{Classicalization Parameters}_i, \text{Efficiency}_i\right)

M_i = \int_{t_0}^{t_1} O_i(\Psi_{\text{Quantum}}) \, dt
