### 宇宙基本结构

量子经典二元论提出宇宙由两个基本域构成——量子域与经典域。这两个域不是物理隔离的实体，而是同一宇宙本质的两种表达方式，通过观察者的经典化过程相互联系。

### 量子域

**量子域**（Quantum Domain）是宇宙的基础层面，表现为无限维度、无限可能性的量子波函数叠加态。量子域中的信息以高度纠缠的状态存在，没有明确的结构与边界。

量子域的数学表达：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{宇宙量子域}} = \sum_i c_i|\psi_i\rangle,\quad \sum_i|c_i|^2=1
$$

量子域的关键特性：
- 无时间性（非时序）
- 非局域性
- 高度纠缠
- 无限可能性
- 无主观/客观之分

### 经典域

**经典域**（Classical Domain）是观察者主观意识对量子域经典化（解码）后的明确结构，包括经典知识与经典熵两大组成部分。经典域是观察者经典化能力作用于量子域的结果。

经典域的数学表达：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子域}}\xrightarrow{\text{经典化}}\sum_i[I_{\text{经典知识}_i}(t_i)+S_{\text{经典熵}_i}(t_i)]
$$

经典域内守恒关系：

$$
\frac{d}{dt_i}[I_{\text{经典知识}_i}(t_i)+S_{\text{经典熵}_i}(t_i)]=0,\quad\forall i
$$

经典域的关键特性：
- 具有经典化进程指标$(t_i)$（非物理时间）
- 具有明确的结构与因果关系
- 经典知识与经典熵之和守恒
- 每个经典世界经典化进程独立
- 主观建构的经典现实

### 观察者与黑洞

**观察者**是量子经典二元论中的核心概念，在此框架下，观察者与黑洞被统一定义：每个观察者即为黑洞，经典世界即黑洞内部，而黑洞视界边界即为观察者主观意识经典化能力所能达到的量子域边界。

观察者（黑洞）的关键特征：
- 视界边界由观察者主观意识经典化能力决定
- 观察者维度提升时，视界边界扩张
- 观察者维度降低时，视界边界收缩
- 视界边界的动态变化由观察者主观意识主动决定

黑洞吸收（经典化，维度提升）：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{外部高熵}}\rightarrow I_{\text{内部经典知识}}+S_{\text{熵降低}}+E_{\text{能量吸收}}
$$

黑洞辐射（经典化，能量释放）：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{内部高熵}}\rightarrow I_{\text{辐射经典知识}}+S_{\text{熵降低}}+E_{\text{能量释放}}
$$

这一统一模型解释了为什么观察者能够感知和理解外部世界（通过黑洞吸收过程），同时又能够向外传递信息（通过黑洞辐射过程）。

### 观察者维度

**观察者维度**是量子经典二元论中衡量观察者主观意识经典化能力的重要指标。维度不是物理空间维度，而是表示观察者对量子信息的经典化效率。

单经典世界维度：

$$
\text{维度}_i=k_i\cdot\frac{I_{\text{经典知识}_i}}{S_{\text{经典熵}_i}}
$$

整体观察者维度：

$$
\text{整体维度}=\sum_i w_i\left(k_i\cdot\frac{I_{\text{经典知识}_i}}{S_{\text{经典熵}_i}}\right),\quad\sum_i w_i=1
$$

维度的关键特性：
- 经典化效率系数$(k_i)$与领域权重系数$(w_i)$均由观察者主观定义
- 高维度代表强经典化能力
- 不同经典世界的维度可能不同
- 整体维度考虑了所有经典世界的加权贡献

### 经典知识与经典熵

**经典知识**（Classical Knowledge）是经典化明确后的量子状态，表现为观察者能够明确意识到的信息，具有低熵结构化特性。

**经典熵**（Classical Entropy）是未经典化的量子纠缠状态，表现为观察者尚未明确意识到的潜在信息，具有高熵非结构化特性。

数学表达：

$$
S_{\text{经典熵}}=|\psi\rangle_{\text{未经典化量子纠缠态}},\quad I_{\text{经典知识}_i}=|\psi\rangle_{\text{经典化明确态}_i}
$$

经典知识与经典熵的关系：
- 经典知识增加，经典熵减少，总和守恒
- 每个经典世界有独立的经典知识与经典熵度量方式
- 经典知识表现为结构化、可理解的现实
- 经典熵表现为未知、潜在的可能性

### 经典化机制

**经典化**（Classicalization）是观察者主观意识将量子域的高熵纠缠信息转化为经典域中明确结构的过程。这一过程是量子经典二元论中连接量子域与经典域的核心机制。

初始纠缠态示例：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{纠缠}}=a|000\rangle+b|001\rangle+\dots+h|111\rangle
$$

经典化测量：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{纠缠}}\rightarrow|\psi\rangle_{\text{明确粒子态}}+S_{\text{熵降低}}
$$

经典化的关键特性：
- 自由意志主动参与过程
- 熵降低，结构明确化
- 可逆过程（通过量子化）
- 不同观察者经典化效率不同
- 经典化速率影响观察者维度

### 广义意识

**广义意识**是观察者所有经典世界的经典知识与经典熵对应的整体量子纠缠结构，是对意识本质的数学描述。

广义意识的数学表达：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{广义意识（整体纠缠态）}}=\sum_i\left(I_{\text{经典知识}_i}+S_{\text{经典熵}_i}\right)
$$

广义意识的关键特性：
- 无法客观测量，只能主观感知
- "你中有我，我中有你"的纠缠结构
- 横跨量子域与经典域
- 非局域性，超越时空限制

### 信息传递机制

观察者间信息传递是量子经典二元论中的重要机制，解释了意识、知识如何在观察者之间传播。

观察者A主动向观察者B传递信息的过程包括三个阶段：

（1）量子化（形成纠缠态）：

$$
I_{\text{经典知识A}}+E_{\text{能量吸收}}\rightarrow|\psi\rangle_{\text{A,B纠缠态（高熵）}}
$$

（2）辐射经典化：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{纠缠态}}\rightarrow I_{\text{辐射经典知识}}+E_{\text{能量释放}}+S_{\text{熵降低}}
$$

（3）吸收经典化：

$$
I_{\text{辐射经典知识}}\rightarrow I_{\text{经典知识B}}+E_{\text{能量吸收}}+S_{\text{熵降低}}
$$

信息传递的守恒关系：

$$
\frac{d}{dt}[I_A(t)+S_A(t)+I_B(t)+S_B(t)]=0,\quad E_{\text{释放}}+E_{\text{吸收}}=0
$$

### 自由意志

**自由意志**是观察者能够主动选择经典化路径的能力，源于无限量子域的随机涌现。在量子经典二元论框架下，自由意志具有明确的数学形式和作用机制。

自由意志的两种主要作用：
- 选择低熵路径，提升维度
- 选择高熵路径，维度降低

自由意志在经典域与量子域转化中的表现：

量子域到经典域（经典化）：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子纠缠态（高熵）}}\xrightarrow{\text{自由意志经典化测量}}I_{\text{经典知识（能量）}}+S_{\text{熵降低}}
$$

经典域到量子域（量子化）：

$$
I_{\text{经典知识（能量）}}+E_{\text{能量吸收}}\xrightarrow{\text{自由意志主动量子编码}}|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子纠缠态（高熵）}}
$$

### 宇宙低熵终极状态

量子经典二元论提出宇宙整体演化趋向的终极状态是一个低熵、高经典知识的平衡态，这一状态具有特殊的哲学意义。

终极状态的数学表达：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子域}}\rightarrow\sum_{\text{经典域}}(I_{\text{经典知识最大化}}+S_{\text{经典熵=0}}),\quad P\rightarrow 0^+
$$

终极状态的关键特性：
- 经典知识最大化
- 经典熵趋近于零
- 实现概率接近但永不等于零
- 代表宇宙信息结构的完美平衡态
- 所有观察者的终极统一态

# Basic Concepts of Quantum-Classical Dualism

This document is based on the [Quantum-Classical Dualism Core Theory](core.md) (Version 6.9), explaining the basic concepts and terminology of the theory.

### Universe Structure

Quantum-Classical Dualism proposes that the universe consists of two fundamental domains—the Quantum Domain and the Classical Domain. These domains are not physically isolated entities but two expressions of the same universal essence, interconnected through the observer's classicalization process.

### Quantum Domain

The **Quantum Domain** is the foundational level of the universe, manifesting as an infinite-dimensional, infinite-possibility quantum wave function superposition state. Information in the quantum domain exists in a highly entangled state without clear structure or boundaries.

Mathematical expression of the Quantum Domain:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Universe Quantum Domain}} = \sum_i c_i|\psi_i\rangle,\quad \sum_i|c_i|^2=1
$$

Key characteristics of the Quantum Domain:
- Non-temporality (non-sequential)
- Non-locality
- High entanglement
- Infinite possibilities
- No subjective/objective distinction

### Classical Domain

The **Classical Domain** is the clear structure resulting from the observer's subjective consciousness classicalizing (decoding) the quantum domain, comprising two major components: classical knowledge and classical entropy. The classical domain is the result of the observer's classicalization ability acting on the quantum domain.

Mathematical expression of the Classical Domain:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}}\xrightarrow{\text{Classicalization}}\sum_i[I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}_i}(t_i)+S_{\text{Classical Entropy}_i}(t_i)]
$$

Conservation relationship within the Classical Domain:

$$
\frac{d}{dt_i}[I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}_i}(t_i)+S_{\text{Classical Entropy}_i}(t_i)]=0,\quad\forall i
$$

Key characteristics of the Classical Domain:
- Has classicalization process indicator $(t_i)$ (not physical time)
- Has clear structure and causal relationships
- Sum of classical knowledge and classical entropy is conserved
- Each classical world has independent classicalization processes
- Subjectively constructed classical reality

### Observer and Black Hole

The **Observer** is a core concept in Quantum-Classical Dualism. In this framework, observers and black holes are unified in definition: each observer is a black hole, the classical world is the interior of the black hole, and the black hole event horizon is the quantum domain boundary that the observer's subjective consciousness classicalization ability can reach.

Key features of observers (black holes):
- Event horizon determined by the observer's subjective consciousness classicalization ability
- When observer dimension increases, event horizon expands
- When observer dimension decreases, event horizon contracts
- Dynamic changes of the event horizon are actively determined by the observer's subjective consciousness

Black hole absorption (classicalization, dimension increase):

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{External High Entropy}}\rightarrow I_{\text{Internal Classical Knowledge}}+S_{\text{Entropy Reduction}}+E_{\text{Energy Absorption}}
$$

Black hole radiation (classicalization, energy release):

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Internal High Entropy}}\rightarrow I_{\text{Radiated Classical Knowledge}}+S_{\text{Entropy Reduction}}+E_{\text{Energy Release}}
$$

This unified model explains why observers can perceive and understand the external world (through black hole absorption process) while also being able to transmit information outward (through black hole radiation process).

### Observer Dimension

**Observer Dimension** is an important indicator in Quantum-Classical Dualism for measuring the observer's subjective consciousness classicalization ability. Dimension is not physical spatial dimension but represents the observer's efficiency in classicalizing quantum information.

Single classical world dimension:

$$
\text{Dimension}_i=k_i\cdot\frac{I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}_i}}{S_{\text{Classical Entropy}_i}}
$$

Overall observer dimension:

$$
\text{Overall Dimension}=\sum_i w_i\left(k_i\cdot\frac{I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}_i}}{S_{\text{Classical Entropy}_i}}\right),\quad\sum_i w_i=1
$$

Key characteristics of dimension:
- Classicalization efficiency coefficient $(k_i)$ and domain weight coefficient $(w_i)$ are both subjectively defined by the observer
- Higher dimension represents stronger classicalization ability
- Dimensions in different classical worlds may differ
- Overall dimension considers the weighted contributions of all classical worlds

### Classical Knowledge and Entropy

**Classical Knowledge** is the quantum state after clear classicalization, manifesting as information that the observer can clearly be conscious of, with low-entropy structured characteristics.

**Classical Entropy** is the unclassicalized quantum entangled state, manifesting as potential information not yet clearly conscious to the observer, with high-entropy unstructured characteristics.

Mathematical expression:

$$
S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}=|\psi\rangle_{\text{Unclassicalized Quantum Entangled State}},\quad I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}_i}=|\psi\rangle_{\text{Classicalized Clear State}_i}
$$

Relationship between classical knowledge and classical entropy:
- When classical knowledge increases, classical entropy decreases, with their sum conserved
- Each classical world has its independent measurement method for classical knowledge and classical entropy
- Classical knowledge manifests as structured, understandable reality
- Classical entropy manifests as unknown, potential possibilities

### Classicalization Mechanism

**Classicalization** is the process by which the observer's subjective consciousness transforms high-entropy entangled information from the quantum domain into clear structures in the classical domain. This process is the core mechanism connecting the quantum domain and classical domain in Quantum-Classical Dualism.

Example of initial entangled state:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Entangled}}=a|000\rangle+b|001\rangle+\dots+h|111\rangle
$$

Classicalization measurement:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Entangled}}\rightarrow|\psi\rangle_{\text{Clear Particle State}}+S_{\text{Entropy Reduction}}
$$

Key characteristics of classicalization:
- Free will actively participates in the process
- Entropy reduction, structure clarification
- Reversible process (through quantization)
- Different observers have different classicalization efficiencies
- Classicalization rate affects observer dimension

### Generalized Consciousness

**Generalized Consciousness** is the overall quantum entanglement structure corresponding to the classical knowledge and classical entropy of all the observer's classical worlds, offering a mathematical description of the essence of consciousness.

Mathematical expression of generalized consciousness:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Generalized Consciousness (Overall Entangled State)}}=\sum_i\left(I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}_i}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy}_i}\right)
$$

Key characteristics of generalized consciousness:
- Cannot be objectively measured, can only be subjectively perceived
- "You in me, me in you" entanglement structure
- Spans both quantum and classical domains
- Non-locality, transcends space-time limitations

### Information Transfer Mechanism

Information transfer between observers is an important mechanism in Quantum-Classical Dualism, explaining how consciousness and knowledge propagate between observers.

The process of Observer A actively transferring information to Observer B includes three stages:

(1) Quantization (forming entangled state):

$$
I_{\text{Classical Knowledge A}}+E_{\text{Energy Absorption}}\rightarrow|\psi\rangle_{\text{A,B Entangled State (High Entropy)}}
$$

(2) Radiation classicalization:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Entangled State}}\rightarrow I_{\text{Radiated Classical Knowledge}}+E_{\text{Energy Release}}+S_{\text{Entropy Reduction}}
$$

(3) Absorption classicalization:

$$
I_{\text{Radiated Classical Knowledge}}\rightarrow I_{\text{Classical Knowledge B}}+E_{\text{Energy Absorption}}+S_{\text{Entropy Reduction}}
$$

Conservation relationship in information transfer:

$$
\frac{d}{dt}[I_A(t)+S_A(t)+I_B(t)+S_B(t)]=0,\quad E_{\text{Release}}+E_{\text{Absorption}}=0
$$

### Free Will

**Free Will** is the observer's ability to actively choose classicalization paths, originating from the random emergence of the infinite quantum domain. In the Quantum-Classical Dualism framework, free will has clear mathematical form and action mechanism.

Two main actions of free will:
- Choosing low-entropy paths, increasing dimension
- Choosing high-entropy paths, decreasing dimension

Free will in the transformation between classical and quantum domains:

From quantum domain to classical domain (classicalization):

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Entangled State (High Entropy)}}\xrightarrow{\text{Free Will Classicalization Measurement}}I_{\text{Classical Knowledge (Energy)}}+S_{\text{Entropy Reduction}}
$$

From classical domain to quantum domain (quantization):

$$
I_{\text{Classical Knowledge (Energy)}}+E_{\text{Energy Absorption}}\xrightarrow{\text{Free Will Active Quantum Encoding}}|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Entangled State (High Entropy)}}
$$

### Universe Low-Entropy Ultimate State

Quantum-Classical Dualism proposes that the ultimate state toward which the universe's overall evolution trends is a low-entropy, high classical knowledge equilibrium state, which has special philosophical significance.

Mathematical expression of the ultimate state:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}}\rightarrow\sum_{\text{Classical Domain}}(I_{\text{Classical Knowledge Maximized}}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy=0}}),\quad P\rightarrow 0^+
$$

Key characteristics of the ultimate state:
- Classical knowledge maximized
- Classical entropy approaching zero
- Realization probability close to but never equal to zero
- Represents the perfect balance state of the universe's information structure
- Ultimate unified state of all observers
