# 阿尔伯特·爱因斯坦 | Albert Einstein

## 基本信息 | Basic Information

**生卒年份 | Lifespan**: 1879年-1955年 (1879-1955)  
**国籍 | Nationality**: 出生于德国，后成为瑞士公民，最终加入美国国籍 (Born in Germany, later Swiss, finally American)  
**主要领域 | Main Fields**: 理论物理学、量子力学、相对论 (Theoretical Physics, Quantum Mechanics, Relativity)  
**代表作品 | Representative Works**: 《相对论：狭义与广义》、关于光电效应的论文 (Relativity: The Special and General Theory, Papers on Photoelectric Effect)

## 核心思想 | Core Ideas

### 中文

1. **狭义相对论**: 时间和空间是相互关联的，随观察者参考系统不同而变化。
2. **广义相对论**: 引力是由质量引起的时空弯曲。
3. **光量子理论**: 光由离散粒子（光子）组成，为量子力学奠定基础。
4. **质能等价**: 质量与能量可相互转化（E=mc²）。
5. **思想实验**: 通过假设性场景进行理论推导。
6. **决定论**: "上帝不掷骰子"，对量子力学概率解释的不认同。

### English

1. **Special Relativity**: Time and space are interconnected and vary depending on the observer's reference frame.
2. **General Relativity**: Gravity is the curvature of spacetime caused by mass.
3. **Light Quantum Theory**: Light consists of discrete particles (photons), laying foundation for quantum mechanics.
4. **Mass-Energy Equivalence**: Mass and energy are interconvertible (E=mc²).
5. **Thought Experiments**: Theoretical derivation through hypothetical scenarios.
6. **Determinism**: "God does not play dice," expressing disagreement with the probabilistic interpretation of quantum mechanics.
