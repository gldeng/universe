$$
\Omega_{Q}^{VR} \subset \Omega_{Q}
$$

$$
\mathcal{C}_{VR}: \Omega_{Q}^{VR} \rightarrow \Omega_{C}^{VR}
$$

4. **虚拟观察者** $`\mathcal{O}_{VR}`$：在虚拟现实中执行经典化的实体

$$
\mathcal{O}_{VR} = \{\mathcal{C}_{\mathcal{O}}^{VR}, \mathcal{Q}_{\mathcal{O}}^{VR}, K_{C}^{\mathcal{O},VR}\}
