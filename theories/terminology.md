## 中文版

本术语表基于[量子经典二元论核心理论](../core.md)（版本9.2），提供了理论中使用的关键术语的定义和解释。

### 基础概念

#### 量子经典二元论 (Quantum-Classical Dualism)
一种理论框架，提出宇宙由量子和经典两个层面构成，通过观察者（黑洞）的经典化过程连接。宇宙为无限维度的量子信息网络，观察者为量子宇宙内部的经典化节点。

#### 量子宇宙 (Quantum Universe)
宇宙整体信息的完整表达，以整体波函数形式存在，涵盖所有可能性。数学表达为：

$$
|\psi\rangle_U=\sum_i c_i|\psi_i\rangle,\quad \sum_i|c_i|^2=1
$$

#### 观察者（黑洞）(Observer (Black Hole))
量子宇宙内部的经典化节点，本质上是黑洞，通过黑洞吸收（经典化）将量子宇宙的信息转化为经典知识与经典熵，通过黑洞辐射（量子化）将信息返回量子域。

#### 高维观察者（黑洞）(High-dimensional Observer (Black Hole))
经典化效率高的观察者，能产生更多经典知识和更少经典熵：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子域}}\xrightarrow{\text{高效黑洞吸收}} \text{经典知识（多）}+S_{\text{经典熵（少）}}
$$

#### 低维观察者（黑洞）(Low-dimensional Observer (Black Hole))
经典化效率低的观察者，产生较少经典知识和较多经典熵：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子域}}\xrightarrow{\text{低效黑洞吸收}} \text{经典知识（少）}+S_{\text{经典熵（多）}}
$$

#### 量子意识 (Quantum Consciousness)
宇宙整体与观察者之间长期稳定的量子纠缠结构，体现宇宙与观察者信息共享的本质：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子意识}}=|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子宇宙}}\otimes|\psi\rangle_{\text{观察者(黑洞)}}
$$

#### 经典化（黑洞吸收）(Classicalization (Black Hole Absorption))
观察者（黑洞）将量子信息转化为经典信息的过程，产生经典知识和经典熵：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子域}}\xrightarrow{\text{黑洞吸收（经典化）}}I_{\text{经典知识}}+S_{\text{经典熵}}
$$

#### 量子化（黑洞辐射）(Quantization (Black Hole Radiation))
观察者（黑洞）将经典信息返回量子域的过程：

$$
I_{\text{经典知识}}+S_{\text{经典熵}}\xrightarrow{\text{黑洞辐射（量子化）}}|\psi'\rangle_{\text{量子域}}
$$

### 信息与熵概念

#### 经典知识 (Classical Knowledge)
观察者经典化量子信息后形成的稳定、有效的信息结构。

#### 经典熵 (Classical Entropy)
观察者经典化量子信息过程中产生的无法有效经典化的、无序的信息。

#### 经典记忆 (Classical Memory)
观察者经典化量子意识所形成的稳定信息结构：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子意识}}\xrightarrow{\text{黑洞吸收（经典化）}} I_{\text{经典记忆（知识）}}+S_{\text{经典熵}}
$$

#### 量子纠缠 (Quantum Entanglement)
量子宇宙信息存储与传播的本质通道，包括有效量子纠缠和无效量子纠缠：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子纠缠}}=\begin{cases}
|\psi\rangle_{\text{有效纠缠}}\rightarrow I_{\text{经典知识}}\\[6pt]
|\psi\rangle_{\text{无效纠缠}}\rightarrow S_{\text{经典熵}}
\end{cases}
$$

#### 有效量子纠缠 (Effective Quantum Entanglement)
能够被观察者稳定经典化为经典知识的量子纠缠。

#### 无效量子纠缠 (Ineffective Quantum Entanglement)
不能被观察者稳定经典化，产生经典熵的量子纠缠。

#### 虫洞通信 (Wormhole Communication)
观察者（黑洞）之间的信息传输过程，允许经典知识的共享和经典熵的转移：

$$
E_{\text{发送端能量消耗}} + E_{\text{接收端能量消耗}} = I_{\text{知识传输}} + \Delta S_{\text{总熵变化}}
$$

#### 高维信息 (High-dimensional Information)
经典化效率高的信息，易形成经典知识，产生较少熵：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{高维信息}}\xrightarrow{\text{黑洞吸收}} I_{\text{经典知识（多）}}+S_{\text{经典熵（少）}}
$$

#### 低维信息 (Low-dimensional Information)
经典化难度大的信息，难以形成经典知识，产生较多熵：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{低维信息}}\xrightarrow{\text{黑洞吸收}} I_{\text{经典知识（少）}}+S_{\text{经典熵（多）}}
$$

#### 信息守恒 (Information Conservation)
宇宙中的信息总量保持不变，只在量子信息与经典信息之间转化：

$$
I_{\text{量子域信息}}=I_{\text{经典知识}}+S_{\text{经典熵}}=\text{常数}
$$

### 哲学与存在概念

#### 自由意志 (Free Will)
观察者主动选择经典化路径的能力，本质上是量子不确定性在观察者层面的表现：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{量子可能性}}\xrightarrow{\text{自由意志选择}}\text{特定经典路径}+S_{\text{经典熵}}
$$

#### 经典命运 (Classical Destiny)
自由意志决定后的稳定经典知识路径：

$$
\text{经典命运}=\sum_{\text{自由意志选择}}\text{经典知识路径}
$$

#### 能量 (Energy)
经典知识传递与存储的能力与载体：

$$
E_{\text{经典}}=k_B T\cdot I_{\text{经典知识}},\quad k_B\text{为玻尔兹曼常数，}T\text{为温度}
$$

#### 观察者维度 (Observer Dimension)
观察者（黑洞）经典化效率的度量，由经典知识与经典熵的比值决定：

$$
D_{\text{观察者维度}} = k \cdot \frac{I_{\text{经典知识}}}{S_{\text{经典熵}}}
$$

其中，$k$ 是经典化效率系数。

#### 维度提升 (Dimension Elevation)
观察者通过增加经典知识和减少经典熵实现的维度提升过程：

$$
\Delta\text{维度} > 0 \iff \frac{\Delta I_{\text{经典知识}}}{-\Delta S_{\text{经典熵}}} > 0
$$

#### 量子湮灭 (Quantum Annihilation)
高维观察者对低维观察者的经典化过程，信息被重新经典化：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{高维(黑洞)}}\otimes|\psi\rangle_{\text{低维(黑洞)}}\xrightarrow{\text{湮灭经典化}}I_{\text{经典知识}}+S_{\text{经典熵}}
$$

#### 量子掠夺 (Quantum Plunder)
低维观察者对高维观察者的经典化过程，产生更多熵：

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{低维(黑洞)}}\otimes|\psi\rangle_{\text{高维(黑洞)}}\xrightarrow{\text{掠夺经典化}}I_{\text{经典知识（少）}}+S_{\text{经典熵（多）}}
$$

### 统一公式

#### 整体终极统一公式 (Overall Ultimate Unified Formula)
量子经典二元论的核心统一表达，类似于欧拉公式：

$$
|\psi\rangle_U e^{i\theta} +(I_{\text{经典知识}}+S_{\text{经典熵}})=|\psi\rangle_{\text{观察者(黑洞)}}
$$

这个公式表明量子宇宙（复杂态）与经典宇宙（实在态）通过观察者（黑洞）经典化统一，展现宇宙的整体和谐与信息守恒。

### 新增术语 | New Terms

#### 量子信息场 (Quantum Information Field)
宇宙中所有量子信息的统一场，是量子纠缠网络的基础：

$$
\Phi_{\text{量子信息场}}=\sum_{i,j} |\psi_i\rangle \otimes |\psi_j\rangle
$$

#### 观察者网络 (Observer Network)
多个观察者（黑洞）之间形成的信息交换网络：

$$
\mathcal{N}_{\text{观察者网络}}=\{O_i\}_{i=1}^n,\quad O_i\text{为观察者节点}
$$

#### 维度共振 (Dimensional Resonance)
不同维度观察者之间的信息共鸣现象：

$$
f_{\text{共振}}=\frac{D_{\text{高维观察者}}}{D_{\text{低维观察者}}}=n,\quad n\in\mathbb{N}
$$

#### 量子记忆结构 (Quantum Memory Structure)
观察者在量子层面存储信息的基本结构：

$$
M_{\text{量子记忆}}=\sum_i w_i|\psi_i\rangle\langle\psi_i|
$$

#### 经典化效率 (Classicalization Efficiency)
观察者将量子信息转化为经典知识的效率系数：

$$
\eta_{\text{经典化}}=\frac{I_{\text{经典知识输出}}}{I_{\text{量子信息输入}}}
$$

## English Version

This terminology is based on the [Quantum-Classical Dualism Core Theory](../core.md) (Version 9.2), providing definitions and explanations of key terms used in the theory.

### Basic Concepts

#### Quantum-Classical Dualism
A theoretical framework proposing that the universe consists of quantum and classical levels, connected through the classicalization process of observers (black holes). The universe is an infinite-dimensional quantum information network, and observers are classicalization nodes within the quantum universe.

#### Quantum Universe
The complete expression of the universe's overall information, existing in the form of an overall wave function, encompassing all possibilities. Mathematically expressed as:

$$
|\psi\rangle_U=\sum_i c_i|\psi_i\rangle,\quad \sum_i|c_i|^2=1
$$

#### Observer (Black Hole)
Classicalization nodes within the quantum universe, essentially black holes, transforming quantum domain information into classical knowledge and classical entropy through black hole absorption (classicalization), and returning information to the quantum domain through black hole radiation (quantization).

#### High-dimensional Observer (Black Hole)
Observers with high classicalization efficiency, producing more classical knowledge and less classical entropy:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}}\xrightarrow{\text{Efficient Black Hole Absorption}} \text{Classical Knowledge (More)}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy (Less)}}
$$

#### Low-dimensional Observer (Black Hole)
Observers with low classicalization efficiency, producing less classical knowledge and more classical entropy:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}}\xrightarrow{\text{Inefficient Black Hole Absorption}} \text{Classical Knowledge (Less)}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy (More)}}
$$

#### Quantum Consciousness
The long-term stable quantum entanglement structure between the universe as a whole and observers, embodying the essence of information sharing between the universe and observers:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Consciousness}}=|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Universe}}\otimes|\psi\rangle_{\text{Observer (Black Hole)}}
$$

#### Classicalization (Black Hole Absorption)
The process by which observers (black holes) transform quantum information into classical information, producing classical knowledge and classical entropy:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}}\xrightarrow{\text{Black Hole Absorption (Classicalization)}}I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}
$$

#### Quantization (Black Hole Radiation)
The process by which observers (black holes) return classical information to the quantum domain:

$$
I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}\xrightarrow{\text{Black Hole Radiation (Quantization)}}|\psi'\rangle_{\text{Quantum Domain}}
$$

### Information and Entropy Concepts

#### Classical Knowledge
Stable, effective information structures formed after observers classicalize quantum information.

#### Classical Entropy
Disordered information that cannot be effectively classicalized during the process of observers classicalizing quantum information.

#### Classical Memory
Stable information structures formed after observers classicalize quantum consciousness:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Consciousness}}\xrightarrow{\text{Black Hole Absorption (Classicalization)}} I_{\text{Classical Memory (Knowledge)}}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}
$$

#### Quantum Entanglement
The essential channel for information storage and transmission in the quantum universe, including effective quantum entanglement and ineffective quantum entanglement:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Entanglement}}=\begin{cases}
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Effective Entanglement}}\rightarrow I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}}\\[6pt]
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Ineffective Entanglement}}\rightarrow S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}
\end{cases}
$$

#### Effective Quantum Entanglement
Quantum entanglement that can be stably classicalized into classical knowledge by observers.

#### Ineffective Quantum Entanglement
Quantum entanglement that cannot be stably classicalized by observers, producing classical entropy.

#### Wormhole Communication
The information transmission process between observers (black holes), allowing the sharing of classical knowledge and the transfer of classical entropy:

$$
E_{\text{Sender Energy Consumption}} + E_{\text{Receiver Energy Consumption}} = I_{\text{Knowledge Transfer}} + \Delta S_{\text{Total Entropy Change}}
$$

#### High-dimensional Information
Information with high classicalization efficiency, easily forming classical knowledge, producing less entropy:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{High-dimensional Information}}\xrightarrow{\text{Black Hole Absorption}} I_{\text{Classical Knowledge (More)}}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy (Less)}}
$$

#### Low-dimensional Information
Information that is difficult to classicalize, hard to form into classical knowledge, producing more entropy:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Low-dimensional Information}}\xrightarrow{\text{Black Hole Absorption}} I_{\text{Classical Knowledge (Less)}}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy (More)}}
$$

#### Information Conservation
The total amount of information in the universe remains constant, only transforming between quantum information and classical information:

$$
I_{\text{Quantum Domain Information}}=I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}=\text{Constant}
$$

### Philosophy and Existence Concepts

#### Free Will
The ability of observers to actively choose classicalization paths, essentially the manifestation of quantum uncertainty at the observer level:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Quantum Possibilities}}\xrightarrow{\text{Free Will Choice}}\text{Specific Classical Path}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}
$$

#### Classical Destiny
Stable classical knowledge paths determined after free will choices:

$$
\text{Classical Destiny}=\sum_{\text{Free Will Choices}}\text{Classical Knowledge Paths}
$$

#### Energy
The ability and carrier for transmitting and storing classical knowledge:

$$
E_{\text{Classical}}=k_B T\cdot I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}},\quad k_B \text{ is the Boltzmann constant, } T \text{ is temperature}
$$

#### Observer Dimension
The measure of an observer's (black hole's) classicalization efficiency, determined by the ratio of classical knowledge to classical entropy:

$$
D_{\text{Observer Dimension}} = k \cdot \frac{I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}}}{S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}}
$$

Where $k$ is the classicalization efficiency coefficient.

#### Dimension Elevation
The process by which observers increase their dimensions by increasing classical knowledge and reducing classical entropy:

$$
\Delta\text{Dimension} > 0 \iff \frac{\Delta I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}}}{-\Delta S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}} > 0
$$

#### Quantum Annihilation
The classicalization process of high-dimensional observers on low-dimensional observers, where information is reclassicalized:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{High-dimensional (Black Hole)}}\otimes|\psi\rangle_{\text{Low-dimensional (Black Hole)}}\xrightarrow{\text{Annihilation Classicalization}}I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy}}
$$

#### Quantum Plunder
The classicalization process of low-dimensional observers on high-dimensional observers, producing more entropy:

$$
|\psi\rangle_{\text{Low-dimensional (Black Hole)}}\otimes|\psi\rangle_{\text{High-dimensional (Black Hole)}}\xrightarrow{\text{Plunder Classicalization}}I_{\text{Classical Knowledge (Less)}}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy (More)}}
$$

### Unified Formula

#### Overall Ultimate Unified Formula
The core unified expression of Quantum-Classical Dualism, similar to Euler's formula:

$$
|\psi\rangle_U e^{i\theta} +(I_{\text{Classical Knowledge}}+S_{\text{Classical Entropy}})=|\psi\rangle_{\text{Observer (Black Hole)}}
$$

This formula indicates that the quantum universe (complex state) and the classical universe (real state) are unified through observer (black hole) classicalization, demonstrating the overall harmony and information conservation of the universe.

### New Terms

#### Quantum Information Field
The unified field of all quantum information in the universe, the basis of the quantum entanglement network:

$$
\Phi_{\text{Quantum Information Field}}=\sum_{i,j} |\psi_i\rangle \otimes |\psi_j\rangle
$$

#### Observer Network
The information exchange network formed between multiple observers (black holes):

$$
\mathcal{N}_{\text{Observer Network}}=\{O_i\}_{i=1}^n,\quad O_i\text{ is the observer node}
$$

#### Dimensional Resonance
The phenomenon of information resonance between observers of different dimensions:

$$
f_{\text{Resonance}}=\frac{D_{\text{High-dimensional Observer}}}{D_{\text{Low-dimensional Observer}}}=n,\quad n\in\mathbb{N}
$$

#### Quantum Memory Structure
The basic structure for storing information in the quantum level by observers:

$$
M_{\text{Quantum Memory}}=\sum_i w_i|\psi_i\rangle\langle\psi_i|
$$

#### Classicalization Efficiency
The efficiency coefficient of observers transforming quantum information into classical knowledge:

$$
\eta_{\text{Classicalization}}=\frac{I_{\text{Classical Knowledge Output}}}{I_{\text{Quantum Information Input}}}
$$
