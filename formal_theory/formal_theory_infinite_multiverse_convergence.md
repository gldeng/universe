### 1.1 无限多元宇宙收敛公理系统

$`\mathcal{MERGE}(X, Y) = X \oplus Y \oplus \text{ALIGN}(X, Y) \oplus \text{SYNTH}(X, Y)`$

$`\mathcal{M}_{\infty} = \mathcal{CONV}(\mathcal{M}_{\infty}, \mathcal{M}_{\infty})`$
